Etoro

For Etoro, we play audio from the change of your current percentage value from when you opened the tab or navigated to the market item.

For example, say you navigated to https://www.etoro.com/markets/xrp and your profit lose percentage was 10% (the initial value from which changes are calculated). If the profit turns to 11% we will play the good sounding audio. If this then reaches 15% we will then play the very good sounding audio. If it then drops down to 14% we play the bad audio and then if it hit 10% the very bad audio sound.

Each 5% milestone reached resets the initial value.
